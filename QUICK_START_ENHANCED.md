## How to Use

### Step 1: Generate (Same as Before)
1. Upload room image
2. Select room type + style + budget
3. Click "Generate Design"
4. Wait 3-5 seconds

### Step 2: Review Result
- Check "Before & After" comparison
- Look for "✨ Refine Your Design" panel below

### Step 3: Adjust (NEW!)
Move sliders to fine-tune:
- **AI Design Strength:** How much AI vs original
- **Structure Preservation:** Keep walls/windows
- **Lighting Blend:** Original vs AI lighting
- **Color Intensity:** Vibrant vs subtle
- **Contrast:** Flat vs punchy
- **Brightness:** Dark vs bright

### Step 4: Apply
- Click "Apply Changes"
- Wait 1-2 seconds
- See updated result

### Step 5: Iterate or Download
- Adjust again if needed
- Or click "Reset to Original"
- Download when satisfied

---

## Quick Tips

### For Most Realistic Results:
- Start with default values
- Make small adjustments (5-10% at a time)
- Match brightness to your room's lighting
- Keep structure preservation at 35-50%

### Common Adjustments:

**Too Artificial?**
- Reduce AI Strength to 50%
- Increase Structure to 45%
- Reduce Color Intensity to 30%

**Too Subtle?**
- Increase AI Strength to 70%
- Reduce Structure to 35%
- Increase Color Intensity to 45%

**Too Dark?**
- Increase Brightness to +15%
- Increase Lighting Blend to 45%

**Too Bright?**
- Decrease Brightness to -15%
- Decrease Lighting Blend to 25%

## Troubleshooting

**Edit panel not showing?**
- Check console for errors
- Refresh page and try again

**Changes not applying?**
- Wait for "Apply Changes" button to re-enable
- Check console for errors

**Output still looks artificial?**
- Try reducing AI Strength to 45-55%
- Increase Structure Preservation to 45-55%
- Reduce Color Intensity to 25-35%
